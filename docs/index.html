      What's my version?

      In order to check the version of your product:
      1. Go to your Content Hub instance (e.g. my-instance.com/en-us).
      2. Remove the /en-us from the URL, add /api/status then hit Enter.
      3. In the JSON data, identify the "product_version" property.
      4. The version of the product is the part before the '+' sign of the product_version value. In this example, the value is 3.3.0.
